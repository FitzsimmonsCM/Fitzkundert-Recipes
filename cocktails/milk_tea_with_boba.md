Ingredients
===========
Boba:
- Instant tapioca pearls

Milk Tea:
- 235 g (1 cup) water, 100Â°C
- 5 bags black tea
- 85 g half and half
- 113 g ice

Simple syrup:
- 6 tbsp brown sugar
- 3 tbsp water

Directions
==========
- Warm tapioca pearls as per instructions.

- Steep tea for 4 min.

- Combine tea, boba, and cream.  Add simple syrup to taste.
