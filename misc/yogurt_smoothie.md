Ingredients
===========
- 115 g (1/2 cup) Greek yogurt
- 1-2 cups fruit, frozen or raw, e.g.:
  - blueberry, apple
  - blueberry, strawberry, banana
  - blueberry, cherry
- 1 cup milk

Preparation
===========
- Place yogurt and fruit in a plastic bag and freeze.

Directions
==========
- Combine all ingredients in the bowl of a blender.

- Blend until smooth.
