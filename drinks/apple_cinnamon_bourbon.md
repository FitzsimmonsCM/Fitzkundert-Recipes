Ingredients
===========
- 1 cup (42 g) dried apples
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 350 mL (330 g [1]) bourbon

Directions
==========
- Mix ingredients in a mason jar.

- Infuse at room temperature for 3-5 days.

  - Taste periodically to make sure the cinnamon flavor isn't too strong.  If 
    necessary, remove the cinnamon sticks and continue infusing.

- Filter the infused vodka.  Squeeze the apples a bit to wring out as much 
  vodka as possible.

Notes
=====
[1] Assuming a density of 0.94 g/mL.
