Baked Brie
=====
Time = 5 min prep + 15 min bake\
Servings = 1 wheel of dip \
From: cheezy talk with madelyn

Ingredients
----
- 1 small wheel of bree 
- candied nuts, rough chopped
- fig jam
- thyme


Directions
----

1. Score the brie in a diamond cross-hatch pattern

2. Spread 1-2 spoonfuls of fig jam evenly over the brie

3. Top with fresh thyme and chopped nuts

4. Bake in oven-safe pan at 350ÂºF for 10-15 min

5. Serve
