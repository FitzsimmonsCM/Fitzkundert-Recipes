Chickpea Israeli Couscous Salad
====
Servings: Approx 4 \
Time: 10 min prep + 1-2 hr chill \
From: The Kichn

Ingredients:
----

**Salad**

- 1 cup couscous (normal or Israeli)
- 1 can chickpeas, rinsed & drained
- 10 ounce container cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2-3 persian cucumbers, chopped (can sub 1 normal cucumber, cut into quarter circles)
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- Optional, 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (o)

**Dressing**
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- 2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar (can sub balsamic)
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon honey
- to taste, salt and pepper 


Directions
----

1. For the dressing, add all the ingredients to a bowl and whisk together. Adjust any seasonings if need be and set aside. 

2. Cook couscous according to package and then set aside until cooled. Add a tiny bit of oil or butter to couscous so it doesnâ€™t stick. 

3. Rinse and dry chickpeas. Mix chickpeas and couscous with dressing and chill for approx 10-20 min. 

4. While couscous is chilling, chop tomatoes, cucumbers, and parsley. 

5. Toss couscous with veggies and feta. Adjust seasoning to your taste with salt, pepper, and lemon. Chill 1-2 additional hours before serving.
